Part Three: The Seashell Environment

** If you did not find magic-number.txt file in your Seashell environment, please download it here.

In CS 136, we have developed an online environment called Seashell in which you can write, test and submit your assignments. To access it, go to this website.

Note: This uses the same environment used to test and evaluate your assignment submissions. We recommend that you use this environment to develop your programs for CS 136. Other environments may behave differently and we will not provide any support for other environments.

This part will help give you a basic introduction to Seashell to get you going.

Once you have the Seashell environment loaded in your webbrowser, download the zip file Seashell-Magic onto your desktop. Create a new folder and unzip the files into the folder.

Follow these steps:
  1. Go into the A0 project in Seashell and click on q3 (you should see the file magic-number.txt already there).
  2. Upload all 5 files into q3 using the Seashell 'ADD FILE' operation.
  3. Click on c-magic-number.c, press the 'RUN' button, enter your student number and record your magic number.
  4. Click on racket-magic-number.rkt, press the 'RUN' button, enter your student number and record your magic number.
  5. Click on magic-number.txt to insert your student number, C magic number (from step 3), and Racket magic number (from step 4).
  6. Click on 'SUBMIT QUESTION' to submit magic-number.txt to marmoset.

Note: edit magic-number.txt in Seashell to ensure that you do not accidentally change the formatting.

This part will help ensure that you have RunC working correctly.
